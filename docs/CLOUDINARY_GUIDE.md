# How to Upload Images (Cloudinary)

1. Go to cloudinary.com and log in
2. Click "Upload" in the top menu
3. Drag and drop your photo(s)
4. Once uploaded, click the photo
5. Copy the URL shown (ends in .jpg or .png)
6. Paste this URL into Keystatic when adding a project or blog post

## Image size guidelines

- Project cover photo: at least 1200 x 800px
- Blog cover photo: at least 1200 x 630px
- Client photo (testimonials): at least 200 x 200px

## How to Upload the Hero Background Video

1. Go to cloudinary.com and log in
2. Click "Upload" in the top menu, choose your video file (MP4, MOV, or WebM)
3. Once uploaded, click the video to open its details
4. Copy the **Public ID** shown (e.g. `hero/construction-timelapse` — does NOT include the file extension)
5. In Keystatic: go to **Company Info** → paste the Public ID into **Hero Background Video ID** → Save
6. The homepage hero will now auto-play the video as a silent background loop

### Video guidelines

- Recommended length: 15–30 seconds (loops seamlessly)
- Recommended resolution: 1920 × 1080 (Full HD) or 1280 × 720 (HD)
- Keep file size under 20 MB for fast loading — Cloudinary will auto-optimise it
- Leave the **Hero Background Video ID** field blank to show only the static background image instead
