# How to Upload Images (Cloudinary)

1. Go to cloudinary.com and log in
2. Click "Upload" in the top menu
3. Drag and drop your photo(s)
4. Once uploaded, click the photo
5. Copy the URL shown (ends in .jpg or .png)
6. Paste this URL into Keystatic when adding a project or blog post

## Image size guidelines
- Project cover photo: at least 1200 x 800px
- Blog cover photo: at least 1200 x 630px
- Client photo (testimonials): at least 200 x 200px
